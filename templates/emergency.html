Basic First Aid Tips

Heart Attack

  • Call ambulance immediately (108)
  • Make person sit or lie down comfortably
  • Loosen tight clothing
  • If conscious, give aspirin if available
  • Keep calm and reassure the person

Burns

  • Cool the burn under running water
  • Do not apply ice directly
  • Cover with clean, dry cloth
  • Do not break blisters
  • Seek medical help for severe burns

Bleeding

  • Apply direct pressure on wound
  • Elevate the injured area
  • Use clean cloth or bandage
  • Do not remove embedded objects
  • Seek medical help if bleeding doesn't stop

Choking

  • Encourage coughing if able to breathe
  • Perform Heimlich maneuver if unable to breathe
  • Stand behind the person
  • Make a fist above the navel
  • Pull inward and upward sharply

Important Notice

In case of a medical emergency, always call the emergency services immediately. The information provided here is for basic guidance only and should not replace professional medical advice. If you or someone else is experiencing a life-threatening emergency, dial 112 or 108 immediately.
